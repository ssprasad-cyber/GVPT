About Department

The MBA in Business Analytics program is a symphony of data and strategy, orchestrating a new generation of leaders who turn information into innovation and insight into impact. In a world driven by data, where every click, transaction, and interaction generate a wealth of information, the ability to decode and harness this data is no longer a luxury—it's a necessity.

The MBA Business Analytics program was started by Gayatri Vidya Parishad College for Degree & P.G. Courses(A) in the year 2022. The objective of this program is to create good leaders who create an impact in the world with logic and values. It's a program where the art of leadership meets the science of data. Here, students are not just trained to understand business concepts; they are equipped to transform those concepts using analytical tools and techniques.

An MBA in Business Analytics here is not just about acquiring technical skills; it's about fostering a mindset of curiosity, critical thinking, and continuous learning. The journey extends beyond the classroom, with opportunities for networking, mentorship, and exposure to the latest industry trends.

The department is located in the 8.7-acre lush green Rushikonda campus with well-ventilated spacious classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, library, play fields, canteen, vehicle parking, gym, staff rooms, waiting halls, auditorium, seminar halls, etc.

This program empowers students to become the architects of this data-driven future and is a journey of transformation, where every data point becomes a stepping stone towards innovation and success.

“Data really powers everything that we do.” — Jeff Weiner

Our Mission And Vision

MISSION :

Transform into a premier management institution in pursuit of global knowledge and pragmatic orientation for grooming leadership, entrepreneurial talent and corporate acumen to nurture socially responsible business leaders.

VISION :

“Provide value based management education to nurture global business leaders.”


Program Outcomes (POs)

PO 1: Apply knowledge of management theories and practices to solve business problems

PO 2: Foster analytical and critical thinking abilities for data based decision making.

PO 3: Ability to develop value based leadership ability.

PO 4: Ability to understand analyse and communicate global, economic, legal and ethical aspects of business.

PO 5: Ability to lead themselves and others in the achievement of organizational goals, contributing effectively to a team environment.

PO 6: Ability to continuously learn and adapt to the dynamics of business and society.

PO 7: Acquire entrepreneurial skills to establish and manage enterprises.


Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

The Educational objectives of the two years MBA program of this institution are:

PEO 1: To impart a general level of competence in management and to orient the students to acquire creative, innovative and entrepreneurial potential.

PEO 2: To equip the students with requisite knowledge, skills, right attitude and orientation towards Continuous Learning and to develop efficient managers.

PEO 3: To prepare them to adapt to a rapidly changing environment by applying their conceptual knowledge and become socially responsible and value driven citizens committed to sustainable development.

PEO 4: Students of this program will be equipped with quantitative and qualitative skills to identify, analyze, design and create business opportunities in a globally dynamic environment.

HOD's Message

“Information is the oil of the 21st century, and analytics is the combustion engine.” – Peter Sondergaard

Welcome to the Department of Master of Business Administration- Business Analytics (MBA-BA),

Department of Management Studies with a Vision “to provide value based management education to nurture global business leaders” is proud to introduce a comprehensive two-year MBA in Business Analytics, commencing in the academic year 2022-23, with a capacity for 60 students. This program, will be delivered over four semesters, integrates foundational management subjects in the first year—including Management Concepts, QT, Econometrics, Finance, Marketing, Human Resource Management, Operations, and Business Analytics. In the second year, students will explore advanced topics such as Statistics for Business Analytics, Decision-Making Analytics, R, Python, AI & Machine Learning, Marketing and Retail Analytics, Financial Analytics, HR Analytics, and Operations and Supply Chain Analytics. The curriculum also includes practical lab sessions, latest tools to enhance hands-on experience along with an internship in iv semester.

Our Business Analytics program offers a robust curriculum that covers key areas such as data analysis, statistical modeling, and predictive analytics. Students will gain the skills needed to extract valuable insights, streamline processes, and helps to make informed business decisions.

Welcome to the Department, where we invite you to explore the exciting intersection of Management Education and Data Analytics.
