About Department

GAYATRI VIDYA PARISHAD COLLEGE FOR DEGREE AND PG COURSES (Autonomous) introduced a professional program in Business Management at masters level, Master of Business Administration (MBA) in 1995 affiliated to Andhra University, Visakhapatnam. The PG Department of Management Studies (MBA) has a special status in the academic stream of GAYATRI VIDYA PARISHAD. It stands as a replica of the vision and mission of the parent organization. The MBA program, Department of Management Studies, has a special status in the academic streams of GAYATRI VIDYA PARISHAD with the aim of producing competent management professionals to meet the requirements of the corporate world. Since day one, the department has been constantly striving to achieve excellence in the field of management education by focusing on qualitative teaching. The department has been successful in adopting innovative methods of imparting management education and nurturing the students to meet the challenges of the industry. The department has been very fortunate in having the expertise of several senior academicians and intellectuals who have been instrumental in shaping the institution to its present status with their vision and teaching. The research work done by faculty of any master’s program contributes significantly to the department and to the college as well. The progress of the management department towards this is highly significant with a total of 12 Doctorates and all faculty members have qualified UGC-NET/SET. The faculty members undertake consultancy assignments at various levels and sectors to support the industry. The Department has been recognized as a research centre by Andhra University to guide Ph.D. scholars The department has Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC), and an Industry Institute Partnership Cell (IIPC) funded by AICTE. There is constant encouragement to the faculty and students to participate in National and International Seminars, workshops and various academic development programs. The Training and Placement Cell (TPC) coordinates training and placement activities of the students. The Department of Management Studies has started offering Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) from 2016-17 admitted batches wherein the students can choose one functional specialization among Marketing, Finance & HR and one sectorial specialization among Retail Management, Banking & Financial Services, Hospital Management & Logistics and Supply Chain Management. Further the department is offering open electives in emerging areas such as Soft Skills, Advanced Excel and French in first semester and Business Analytics, Yoga, Digital Marketing and Tally in second semester. The department has excellent academic infrastructure like smart classrooms, digital library, computer centre, media centre, seminar halls, linguistic lab, etc. Experts from Industry and Parent University are invited to deliver lectures on different courses to share their hands on experience to the students. The management department is located in the 8.7-acre lush green Rushikonda campus with well- ventilated spacious class rooms, well equipped laboratories, library, play fields, canteen, vehicle parking , gym, staff rooms, waiting halls, auditorium, seminar halls, etc

Our Mission And Vision

MISSION :

Transform into a premier management institution in pursuit of global knowledge and pragmatic orientation for grooming leadership, entrepreneurial talent and corporate acumen to nurture socially responsible business leaders.

VISION :

“Provide value based management education to nurture global business leaders.”


Program Outcomes (POs)

  • PO 1: Apply knowledge of management theories and practices to solve business problems.
  • PO 2: Foster analytical and critical thinking abilities for data based decision making.
  • PO 3: Ability to develop value based leadership ability.
  • PO 4: Ability to understand, analyze and communicate global, economic, legal and ethical aspects of business.
  • PO 5: Ability to lead themselves and others in the achievement of organizational goals, contributing effectively to a team environment.
  • PO 6: Ability to continuously learn and adapt to the dynamics of business and society.
  • PO 7: Acquire entrepreneurial skills to establish and manage enterprises.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

The Educational objectives of the two years MBA program of this institution are:

  • PEO 1: To impart a general level of competence in management and to orient the students to acquire creative, innovative and entrepreneurial potential.
  • PEO 2: To equip the students with requisite knowledge, skills, right attitude and orientation towards Continuous Learning and to develop efficient managers.
  • PEO 3: To prepare them to adapt to a rapidly changing environment by applying their conceptual knowledge and become socially responsible and value driven citizens committed to sustainable development.
  • PEO 4: Students of this program will be equipped with quantitative and qualitative skills to identify, analyze, design and create business opportunities in a globally dynamic environment.

HOD's Message

Welcome to the Department of Master of Business Administration (MBA), Department of Management Studies was established in the year 1995 with a Vision “To provide value based management education to nurture global business leaders”.

The Department’s strength lies in well qualified and experienced faculty who always thrive to deliver professional knowledge and impart interpersonal skills for the overall development of students to meet the expectations of the corporate world.

MBA Department offers a two year Management program with a dual specialization consisting of 4 semesters. The curriculum consists of core papers with specialization such as Marketing, Finance and Human Resource Management along with sectoral specializations with electives like Retail Management, Banking and Financial Services and Hospital Management.

The Department has all the necessary infrastructure for carrying academic activities and it also consists of Industry Institution Partnership Cell (IIPC), Entrepreneurship Cell (EDC), English Lab, and Training and Placement cell. The placement cell has a good record of providing placement to students into reputed companies.

Students come from different backgrounds and the Department strives to bring them to a common platform to explore the subject knowledge and overall personality development. Students are exposed to undertake project works both in their specializations and also on contemporary relevant issues during their two year span. We also offer open electives of Excel, Tally, Yoga, Ethics and French, to improve job enhancing skills.

With all these inputs, we always strive to provide value based education for the overall development of student personality. We adopt modern techniques of teaching pedagogy in delivering the subject and also through guest lectures, seminars, workshops, industrial visits and personality development activities.

The Department also encourages students to organize management events like ASTHRA- a Management Student’s meet, and also make them participate in management events elsewhere. With this introduction, the Department welcomes you to join in exploring the new horizons of Management Education.

Dr. M.S.R. Seshagiri
Head of the Department,
Master of Business Administration
